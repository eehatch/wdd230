Fitt's Law

Google

google.com

Google shows Fitt's Law because the distance between where you click to type in your request and the button to search are close to each other. While links that are used less are in further away from your thumbs on the phone.

Rule of Thirds

Shedd Aquarium

https://www.sheddaquarium.org/

The Shedd Aquarium website shows rule of thirds because the website is split into 3 parts! The top is where images are stored to draw in the customer, then a section of important information, then a different structured part that shows the user recent news.

PARC: Contrast

MoPop - Museum of Pop Culture

https://www.mopop.org/

The MoPop website shows contrast through its use of color. THe website is mostly black/darkblue and white except for the button to buy a ticket. Users are drawn to this bright green button! .
